Celine's Hobbies

Hobby On!

A lifelong creative at heart, I enjoy exploring new mediums that blend craft and artistry. My favorite ways to unwind include crocheting, experimenting with watercolor and acrylic painting, and getting lost in a good psychological thriller. Each of these hobbies fuels a sense of curiosity and craftsmanship that carries into everything I create.

My parents are both hobbyists who have explored many mediums over the years. My mom is an avid felter, and my dad creates alcohol ink art and has dabbled in cement sculpture.
