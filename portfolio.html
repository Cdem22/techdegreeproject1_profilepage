Mulray Wine Label

A wine label for Mulray Pinot Noir that blends watercolor art with a shaded relief map of the region where the wine was made. The sun exposure is set to match the date and time of harvest, and a small wine bottle icon marks the location of the 30-acre vineyard.

Programs and skills utilized:

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • QGIS
